Safety at Manticore

We Take Safety Very Seriously

At Manticore, safety is our #1 priority

Manticore Fuels has multi-stage Quality Assurance/Quality Control measures in place to deliver clean product to our customers on a consistent basis.

General Information

From the procurement rack to the customer, you can count on Manticore to get the job done safely and in a timely manner. Our transport trucks are dedicated exclusively to fuel transport, to ensure that no in-transit contamination occurs.

  • We sample every delivery that arrives at our yard immediately and safely, discarding any fuel that is not top quality.
  • Our fuel is transferred by a top-loading process and our equipment is inspected for bubbling, rust, or other potential detrimental factors.

Safety at the Forefront

  • Fuel Technicians undergo extensive safety training, hazmat training, and process reviews to verify knowledge of proper handling.
  • All of our Fuel Technicians are a minimum of 23 years of age and have 3-4 years of prior experience in the field.
