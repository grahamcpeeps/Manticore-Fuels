Our Products and Services


At Manticore Fuels we offer a wide range of oil & gas fuel services in West Texas and New Mexico. Manticore fuels specializes in servicing the energy sector by supplying dyed & clear diesel and servicing drilling rigs and frac jobs in the Permian Basin. We make sure fuel gets delivered on time and at the right site through our exceptional customer service. At Manticore Fuels, we rent and sell fuel tanks, and other related containers to fit our customer’s needs. Our fuel tanks range in size from 500 to 20,000 gallons. Manticore Fuels provides refueling services 24/7/365 to frac sites and operations.

Fleet Fueling

Manticore Fuels is a leading hydraulic fracturing fuel supplier in the Permian Basin. Our on-site fueling services are available to refuel your equipment around the clock. We’re equipped to deliver everything you need including fuel, fuel technicians, on site frac tanks and transportation services.

Manticore Fuels mobile fleet fueling services deliver diesel to your equipment any time - day or night - 24/7. Our bobtail trucks are equipped with hand-held computers, thermal printers and document scanners. Our fleet management software provides complete transparency on trucks, employees and inventory for maximum efficiency and significant customer cost savings.

Drilling Rig Fueling

Manticore Fuels provides drilling rig fuel services in Texas and New Mexico. We deliver fuel to locations in 3,500 - 14,000 gallon quantities. We operate 24 hours a day and always have a contact on call to ensure that when ordering fuel, you will be talking to someone who knows and understands your account and your requirements. In addition to fueling services for drilling rigs, we can also provide mobile storage tanks on location. Manticore Fuels is your “one-stop shop” that keeps your drilling operation up and running.

Automated Fueling

Manticore Fuels has partnered with an industry leading partners to provide an innovative automated fueling solution to meet the ever-growing needs of the frac industry. To learn more about our automated solutions, contact a Manticore representative.

Bulk Fuel Storage and Tank Farm

Manticore Fuels provides fuel storage tanks or trailers for our customers. These trailers range from 500-1,000 gallons and tanks range from 500-12,000 gallons. Having the fuel storage capacity to run your business on emergency operation or to have on-site fuel reserves is critical to your business success. All of our tanks come fully dressed out to ensure immediate usage. We offer pumps, hoses, and nozzles with our tanks. Pumps can be provided for diesel or gasoline products and 110v or 12v depending on power source. Contact us and we will consult with you to determine the fuel tank needs that you have based upon your past fuel consumption and present needs.

On-site Fueling Services and
Mobile Fueling

Manticore Fuels provides our customers with on-site fueling. Our bobtail trucks will arrive at your yard and fuel your equipment. Our company is flexible and can work with many different requests when it comes to on-site fueling.

Manticore Fuels offers on-site Diesel fueling. On site diesel refueling services are necessary for the thousands of truck fleets and diesel vehicles across the nation. We deliver fuel directly to your vehicles or to your storage tanks. Our on-site diesel refueling services will help you improve efficiency and help save unnecessary costs.

Emergency Fueling

At Manticore Fuels we know that natural disasters are unavoidable. When disasters strike, we’re there to provide reliable, around-the-clock generator fueling, on-site fueling and dedicated fuel trucks— anywhere in the U.S.

Manticore Fuels is a leading emergency fuel response service provider. We provide 24/7 generator fueling, on-site fueling and dedicated fuel trucks to companies and government agencies during emergencies including severe weather conditions, hurricanes, tornadoes, floods and other disasters. In the past, we have provided critical fuel supply services to governmental agencies, utilities, communication companies, hospitals, delivery services, grocery stores, restoration and other fleet and equipment operators.

Our dedicated trucks, extensive network and unmatched experience ensure guaranteed fuel to keep your facilities running—no matter what.

Manticore Fuels is a leading emergency fuel response provider to utilities and government agencies.

Manticore Fuels emergency response team provides critical fuel to temporary fueling locations, emergency fleets, employee vehicles, RV’s and generators. From our around-the-clock dispatch center to our comprehensive arsenal of emergency response equipment Manticore delivers whatever you need to any place at any time.

Manticore Fuels is here to help. We are driven to provide best-in-class customer service and premium products with an emphasis on safety.
